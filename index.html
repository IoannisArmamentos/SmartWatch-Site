Feature Highlights

What our product is all about
SMARTWATCH WITH GPS

Uses GPS to track your speed, route and distance so you can see just how far you’ve come.

WATERPROOF SMARTWATCH TO TRACK EVERY MOVE

Track your 24/7 activity, steps, distance, calories burned and sleep. Learn how you live, and then live better.

ASSISTANCE ON THE GO

See notifications and meetings, ask Google for help and respond to messages – all on your wrist.

FREE APP AND WEB SERVICE

Link your Polar Flow account to third party services, such as Strava or MyFitnessPal. Share your training data with your coach or PT with Polar Coach, Polar’s free online tool for coaches.

MUSIC WHILE YOU TRAIN

Control music right from your watch. Go phoneless with any Music Player and up to 32GB of onboard storage.

HEALTH COACHING

Take good care of your mind and body health with smart coaching.

HANDSFREE CALLING

On your wrist, you have what you need to make and take calls. Press once to answer and greet your caller without taking your smartphone from your pocket. You’ll never miss a call.

EXTREME BATTERY LIFE

Enjoy up to a 7-day battery life when connected to your phone.
